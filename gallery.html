Hello, I am Dessie - handcrafter & web developer

Gallery

Like a child my dream was to be an animator, I was loving to paint books with pictures and my relative was knowing exactly what to buy me every time - more books for painting.

I love good stories, I was having a good imagination and deft hands. My mom was making jokes that I can become a famous artist because I was having many unfinished paintings.

From last year I found my passion for handicrafts again. The material I am primarily working with is polymer clay. But I am making not only jewelry from polymer clay but also house ornaments from wood with decoupage technique, I am decorating jars as vases for beautiful buckets from crepe paper and also quilling cards.

I am finding great satisfaction in such creative work and it's also so really stress-relieving.
