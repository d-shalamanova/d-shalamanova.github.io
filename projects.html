Projects

Here you will find some of my projects and I will be happy to share with you the process of creating a website for my clients.

  1. First, I get to know the design idea from my clients whether they have a design or other sites they like so I can understand their preferences. the design also takes into account the information they want to provide to their potential customers.
  2. Once we gather the necessary information, we proceed to the design proposals and their comments. The main priority is intuitive, simple, and functional designs that can provide well-organized information.
  3. Apart from the face of the site that potential buyers see, the other challenge for me is to make a site with intuitive administration so that each client can update the information according to their needs.
  4. The last point was to make the website easily readable on different screen sizes and platforms.
